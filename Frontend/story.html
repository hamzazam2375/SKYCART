Our Story

SkyCart started with a vision — a vision born not in a boardroom, but in a chaotic group chat at 3AM, right after our professor casually announced a "fun little DBMS project."

What began as panic-fueled Googling and excessive caffeine consumption turned into something magical (and slightly buggy). Fueled by borderline tears SkyCart was born.

Our journey hasn't been easy — we've battled primary keys, survived foreign key constraints, and yes, we even tried to normalize our lives to 3NF (didn’t work). But through it all, we built a platform that connects buyers, sellers, and every confused student out there who just wants to pass DBMS.

So if you ever wonder why our code has comments like “don't touch this it works somehow” — just know it came from a place of pure dedication and chaotic brilliance. Welcome to SkyCart. Built with sweat, laughs, and a whole lot of SELECT * FROM pain.
